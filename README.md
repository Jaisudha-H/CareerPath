CareerPath is a web-based platform designed to guide students after Class 10 and Class 12 in making informed academic and career decisions. The system delivers personalized recommendations based on aptitude, interests, and academic performance.
The platform integrates career assessment, structured course-to-career roadmaps, government college directory access, admission and scholarship tracking, and live interaction with teachers and counselors into a centralized guidance solution.

Key Features

-Aptitude and interest-based career assessment
-AI-driven personalized stream and degree recommendations
-Course-to-career progression mapping
-Government college search and eligibility guidance
-Admission and scholarship deadline tracking
-Live interaction with teachers and career counselors

Objective

To provide a structured, data-driven, and accessible digital career guidance system that enables students to make confident and well-informed educational decisions.
